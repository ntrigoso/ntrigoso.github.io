Preheat a grill
Preheat a grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
Mix the ingredients
Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
Start creating the patties and chopping ingredients
Add ground beef, bread crumbs, and mixed ingredients and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties. Once patties are formed, start chopping up the lettuce and tomatoes.
Start grilling
Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
Serve and Enjoy!
Once patties are cooked, combine the patty with bread buns, lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes. Serve hot and enjoy!
Ingredient List
1 large egg
½ teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground black pepper
1 pound ground beef
1 butterhead lettuce
1 large tomato
2 slices of cheese
2 bread buns
